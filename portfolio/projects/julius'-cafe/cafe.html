About Us

Originally coming from a background of finance and nursing, Julius Arroyo decided to enter the culinary world and establish Cafe Joyorra as a place for people kick back, relax, enjoy comforting desserts, and come together as a community. The charm of any good cafe is the combination of quality food and modest, yet cozy decorations. We care for people's comfort and always strive to have everyone at ease! The business' ideology is that if you're not comfortable with your environment, then you can't relax to the fullest. Our goal is to make you feel like you're at home - so much so that you may even be tempted to sleep right in our comforting lounge!
